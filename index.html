Product Rebrand (Pure Leaf)

DATE COMPLETED: March 2025
Project Type: Individual Project
Role: Graphic Designer

This Pure Leaf Re-brand aims to bring the refreshing and natural essence the brand promises their consumers. With the elimination of a harsh black background, slight logo font redesign, addition of the tea splash and flavour visual; this re-brand aligns more accurately with the brand while still retaining brand recognition. Consumers are now able to glance from a far and visually see the flavour of the product, the transparent label also supports the brand narrative of being "real" because you are able to see through the bottle.
